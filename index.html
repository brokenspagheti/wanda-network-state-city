The Network State

A New Model of Governance in the Digital Age

by Nikhil | Department of Computer Science and Engineering

Abstract

The concept of the Network State, proposed by Balaji Srinivasan, represents a radical shift in how societies, governance, and nationhood can be organized in the digital era. This paper explores the philosophical foundations, technological enablers, and relevance of network states in addressing limitations of traditional governance structures.

Keywords: Network State, Digital Governance, Blockchain, Decentralized Autonomous Organizations, Cryptographic Identity
